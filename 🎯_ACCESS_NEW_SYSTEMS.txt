======================================================================
                    🔒 LAB PAYMENT VERIFICATION
======================================================================

ACCESS URLs:

1. PAYMENT VERIFICATION DASHBOARD:
   http://127.0.0.1:8000/hms/lab/payment-verification/
   
   - See all tests awaiting payment verification
   - See recently verified tests
   - Click "Verify Payment" on any test

2. VERIFY SPECIFIC TEST PAYMENT:
   http://127.0.0.1:8000/hms/lab/verify-payment/<lab_result_id>/
   
   - Enter receipt number
   - Search and verify payment
   - Auto-verify and enable release


WORKFLOW:

STEP 1: Lab completes test
  - Results entered
  - Status: Completed

STEP 2: Lab sends to cashier
  - Click "Send to Cashier" button (already added)
  - Patient pays
  - Gets receipt: REC-202511-000123

STEP 3: Lab verifies payment
  - Go to Payment Verification Dashboard
  - Click "Verify Payment"
  - Enter receipt number
  - Click "Search Receipt"
  - System shows receipt details
  - Click "Verify & Continue"
  - Payment verified!

STEP 4: Release results
  - Results can now be printed/released
  - Audit trail complete

ACCESS URLs:

1. PATIENT MEDICAL HISTORY:
   http://127.0.0.1:8000/hms/patient/<patient_id>/medical-history/
   
   - See all visits (newest first)
   - Each visit shows:
     * Date, provider, type
     * Chief complaint
     * Vital signs
     * Diagnoses (ICD codes)
     * Medications prescribed
     * Lab tests ordered & results
     * Procedures performed
     * Clinical notes
   
   - Summary shows:
     * Total visits
     * All diagnoses count
     * All medications count
     * All lab tests count
     * Current medications
     * Chronic conditions

2. MEDICAL TIMELINE:
   http://127.0.0.1:8000/hms/patient/<patient_id>/medical-timeline/
   
   - Visual timeline
   - All events chronologically
   - Color-coded by type


HOW DOCTORS USE IT:

1. Open patient chart
2. Click "Medical History" tab
3. See complete history
4. Review previous medications
5. Check previous lab results
6. See chronic conditions
7. Prescribe based on history
8. Logical continuation of care
