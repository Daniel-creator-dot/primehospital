======================================================================
                        🔄 HOW AUTO-SYNC WORKS
======================================================================

CASHIER PROCESSES PAYMENT:
  1. Cashier creates Payment (Transaction model)
  2. AUTOMATIC TRIGGER: Signal fires
  3. System automatically creates:
     ✓ Revenue entry
     ✓ Receipt voucher  
     ✓ Journal entry (Dr: Cash, Cr: Revenue)
     ✓ General ledger postings
     ✓ AR balance update
  4. All done automatically! No manual accounting needed!

INVOICE CREATED:
  1. System creates Invoice
  2. AUTOMATIC TRIGGER: Signal fires
  3. System automatically creates:
     ✓ Accounts Receivable entry
     ✓ Due date tracking
     ✓ Aging bucket classification
  4. AR starts tracking immediately!

PAYMENT APPLIED TO INVOICE:
  1. Payment linked to invoice
  2. AUTOMATIC UPDATES:
     ✓ Invoice balance reduced
     ✓ AR amount_paid increased
     ✓ AR aging recalculated
     ✓ Revenue recorded
     ✓ Journal entry posted
  5. Everything syncs in real-time!
