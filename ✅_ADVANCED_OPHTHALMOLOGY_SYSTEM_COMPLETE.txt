══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                        CLINICAL REFERENCE VALUES
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

VISUAL ACUITY:
  6/6 or 20/20 = Normal vision
  6/12 or 20/40 = Moderate impairment
  6/60 or 20/200 = Legal blindness threshold
  CF = Counting Fingers
  HM = Hand Movements
  PL = Perception of Light
  NPL = No Perception of Light

INTRAOCULAR PRESSURE (IOP):
  Normal: 12-21 mmHg
  Ocular Hypertension: >21 mmHg
  Glaucoma suspect: >21 mmHg with other findings
  Low IOP: <10 mmHg (post-surgery, trauma, uveitis)

CUP-TO-DISC (C/D) RATIO:
  Normal: 0.3-0.4
  Borderline: 0.5
  Suspicious: 0.6
  Glaucomatous: >0.6
  Asymmetry >0.2 between eyes: Concerning

ARTERY/VEIN (A/V) RATIO:
  Normal: 2:3 (arteries narrower than veins)
  Hypertensive: <2:3 (arteriolar narrowing)
  Diabetic: Venous dilation

PUPIL SIZES:
  Normal (room light): 2-4mm
  Dilated (mydriasis): >5mm
  Constricted (miosis): <2mm
  Pharmacologically dilated: 6-8mm
