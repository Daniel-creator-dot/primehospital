Pharmacy Dispensing Protocol
  1. Patient receives prescription from doctor
  2. Patient goes to Cashier to pay
  3. Cashier collects payment and issues receipt
  4. Patient brings receipt to Pharmacy
  5. Pharmacy scans receipt QR or enters number
  6. System verifies payment
  7. Pharmacy dispenses medication
  8. Pharmacy counsels patient on usage
CRITICAL: Do NOT dispense medication without valid payment receipt!
Tip: Always provide patient counselling about medication usage, side effects, and storage.
