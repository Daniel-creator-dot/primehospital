How transfers work
  • Create: Start from Stores (Transfer out / Receive on a store) or New Transfer below. Choose source and destination stores, then pick items and quantities from the source store’s inventory.
  • Approve: Pending transfers can be approved by an authorised user. Approval confirms the transfer is valid and stock is reserved.
  • Complete: When the items are physically received at the destination, complete the transfer. This deducts quantities from the source store and adds them to the destination (creating the item at destination if needed). Only completed transfers update inventory.
