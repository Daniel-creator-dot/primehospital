                    🚀 HOW TO USE
======================================================================

LAB PAYMENT VERIFICATION:

STEP 1: Complete Lab Test
  - Lab staff enters results
  - Marks as completed

STEP 2: Send to Cashier
  - Click "Send to Cashier" button
  - Patient pays at cashier
  - Receives receipt

STEP 3: Verify Payment
  - Lab opens: /hms/lab/payment-verification/
  - Sees list of awaiting verification
  - Clicks "Verify Payment"
  - Enters receipt number
  - Clicks "Search Receipt"
  - System shows receipt details
  - Confirms and verifies

STEP 4: Release Results
  - Results auto-enabled for release
  - Print report
  - Give to patient


MEDICAL HISTORY FOR DOCTORS:

STEP 1: Open Patient Chart
  - Click patient name
  - Go to patient detail

STEP 2: View Medical History
  - Click "Medical History" tab
  - See complete visit history

STEP 3: Review Previous Visits
  - See each visit in detail
  - All medications
  - All lab results
  - All diagnoses
  - All procedures

STEP 4: Continue Treatment
  - Prescribe based on history
  - Avoid drug interactions
  - Check previous test results
  - Logical continuation
