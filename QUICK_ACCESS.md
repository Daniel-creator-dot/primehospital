## 📱 **How to Access Your System**

### **Option 1: From This Computer**

**Simply open your browser and go to:**
```
http://127.0.0.1:8000/hms/
```

**Or:**
```
http://localhost:8000/hms/
```

---

### **Option 2: From ANY Device on Your Network**

**Step 1: Find Your IP Address**

Open PowerShell/Command Prompt and run:
```powershell
ipconfig
```

Look for your IPv4 Address. It will look like:
```
IPv4 Address: 192.168.1.100
```

**Step 2: Access from ANY device**

On any smartphone, tablet, or computer on the same network:
```
http://192.168.1.100:8000/hms/
```
*(Replace `192.168.1.100` with YOUR actual IP)*
