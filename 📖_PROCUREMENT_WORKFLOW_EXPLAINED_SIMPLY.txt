                        🎯 THE SIMPLE EXPLANATION
======================================================================

IMAGINE THIS SCENARIO:

Your hospital pharmacy is running low on medicine.
How do you order more? Here's the professional way:


STEP 1: SOMEONE NEEDS SOMETHING (Request)
------------------------------------------
Who: Pharmacy Manager
What: "We need 10,000 Paracetamol tablets"
Where: Creates procurement request
URL: http://127.0.0.1:8000/hms/procurement/approval/dashboard/

ANALOGY: Like filling out a purchase request form


STEP 2: BOSS CHECKS IF IT'S REALLY NEEDED (Admin Approval)
-----------------------------------------------------------
Who: Hospital Administrator / Department Head
What: Reviews request and asks:
  - Do we really need this?
  - Is it in the budget?
  - Is the quantity reasonable?
Where: Admin approval page
URL: http://127.0.0.1:8000/hms/procurement/admin/pending/

ANALOGY: Like asking your manager if you can buy office supplies


STEP 3: ACCOUNTS CHECKS THE MONEY (Accounts Approval)
------------------------------------------------------
Who: Accounts Department / Finance Manager
What: Reviews request and asks:
  - Do we have money for this?
  - Is the price fair?
  - Which budget does this come from?

THE MAGIC: When accounts approves, computer automatically:
  ✅ Records we owe the vendor money (Accounts Payable)
  ✅ Records this as an expense (Expense Entry)
  ✅ Creates payment authorization (Payment Voucher)

Where: Accounts approval page
URL: http://127.0.0.1:8000/hms/procurement/accounts/pending/

ANALOGY: Like finance department approving your purchase and 
preparing the payment paperwork automatically


STEP 4: MONEY DEPARTMENT PAYS VENDOR (Payment)
-----------------------------------------------
Who: Finance Officer / Cashier
What: Sees payment voucher and processes bank transfer
Where: Payment vouchers list
URL: http://127.0.0.1:8000/admin/hospital/paymentvoucher/

ANALOGY: Like writing a check or making a bank transfer


STEP 5: GOODS ARRIVE (Receiving)
---------------------------------
Who: Warehouse / Store Manager
What: Receives the medicine and confirms delivery
Where: Procurement request detail
URL: http://127.0.0.1:8000/hms/procurement/approval/{id}/detail/

ANALOGY: Like signing for a delivery


STEP 6: DONE! (Completed)
--------------------------
Status: Completed
Everyone happy: 
  ✅ Pharmacy has medicine
  ✅ Vendor got paid
  ✅ Accounts tracked everything
  ✅ Boss knows what happened


======================================================================
                        👥 WHO DOES WHAT?
======================================================================

ROLE 1: PROCUREMENT STAFF (The Requester)
------------------------------------------
Think of them as: "The people who know what's needed"

Examples:
  - Pharmacy Manager (needs medicine)
  - Lab Supervisor (needs test kits)
  - Ward Nurse (needs supplies)

They can:
  ✅ Create requests
  ✅ List what they need
  ✅ Submit for approval
  ✅ Track their requests

They CANNOT:
  ❌ Approve their own requests
  ❌ See others' budgets
  ❌ Process payments


ROLE 2: ADMINISTRATORS (The First Checker)
-------------------------------------------
Think of them as: "The boss who checks if it's necessary"

Examples:
  - Department Heads
  - Senior Managers
  - Operations Managers

They can:
  ✅ See all submitted requests
  ✅ Approve what's necessary
  ✅ Reject what's not needed
  ✅ Add comments/instructions

They CANNOT:
  ❌ Skip to payment
  ❌ See accounting details
  ❌ Change approved requests


ROLE 3: ACCOUNTS STAFF (The Money People)
------------------------------------------
Think of them as: "The people who handle the money"

Examples:
  - Accounts Manager
  - Finance Manager
  - Budget Controller

They can:
  ✅ See admin-approved requests
  ✅ Check budget availability
  ✅ Approve for payment
  ✅ Trigger automatic accounting (THE MAGIC!)

When they approve:
  🎯 System automatically creates:
     - Record of what we owe (Accounts Payable)
     - Expense record (for reports)
     - Payment authorization (Payment Voucher)

They CANNOT:
  ❌ Bypass admin approval
  ❌ Approve without review


ROLE 4: FINANCE OFFICERS (The Payment Processors)
--------------------------------------------------
Think of them as: "The people who actually pay"

Examples:
  - Finance Officer
  - Chief Cashier
  - Treasury Officer

They can:
  ✅ See approved payment vouchers
  ✅ Process bank transfers
  ✅ Mark payments as completed
  ✅ Update vendor records

They CANNOT:
  ❌ Approve purchases
  ❌ Bypass voucher system
  ❌ Pay without authorization
