                        MRN FORMAT BREAKDOWN
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

PMC Prefix for ALL Patients:

NEW SYSTEM:
  Format: PMC + YEAR + Sequential Number
  Example: PMC2025000028
  Meaning: PMC patient #28 in year 2025

LEGACY SYSTEM:
  Format: PMC-LEG- + 6-digit PID
  Example: PMC-LEG-002021
  Meaning: PMC Legacy patient with original PID 2021

BOTH USE "PMC" PREFIX! ✅
Easy to identify: "LEG" means legacy, no "LEG" means new
