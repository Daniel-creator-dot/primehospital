                        🚀 HOW TO USE
======================================================================

STEP 1: REFRESH LAB DASHBOARD
  Press: Ctrl + Shift + R
  URL: http://127.0.0.1:8000/hms/lab-dashboard/

STEP 2: GO TO ANY TAB
  - Pending Results
  - In Progress
  - Completed Today

STEP 3: YOU'LL SEE
  ✅ Price column: GHS XX.XX
  ✅ "Send to Cashier" button (orange/yellow)
  ✅ "Paid" badge if already paid

STEP 4: CLICK "SEND TO CASHIER"
  - Opens cashier page in new tab
  - Patient details pre-filled
  - Test price auto-populated

STEP 5: CASHIER PROCESSES PAYMENT
  - Select payment method
  - Generate receipt with QR code
  - Email sent to patient
  - Revenue synced to accounting
