======================================================================
                        🎯 HOW IT WORKS
======================================================================

HEAD OF DEPARTMENT CAN:
------------------------

1. CREATE SHIFTS:
   - Assign staff to specific shifts
   - Set shift times (Morning/Afternoon/Night/Day/Custom)
   - Add duties and location
   - Track attendance

2. UPLOAD TIMETABLES:
   - Upload monthly duty rosters (PDF/Excel)
   - Make visible to all department staff
   - Update as needed

3. BULK ASSIGN:
   - Use templates to assign multiple shifts
   - Apply to multiple staff at once
   - Set date ranges

4. APPROVE PROCUREMENT:
   - Review department procurement requests
   - Approve or reject
   - Add comments


STAFF CAN SEE:
--------------

1. TODAY'S SCHEDULE:
   - What shifts they have today
   - Shift times
   - Location
   - Assigned duties

2. WEEKLY VIEW:
   - All shifts for the week
   - Check-in status
   - Upcoming shifts

3. MONTHLY ROSTER:
   - Download department roster
   - See full month schedule

                        📝 EXAMPLE WORKFLOW
======================================================================

SCENARIO: HOD Assigns Nursing Shifts for Next Week

STEP 1: HOD Logs In
  - User: Vida Blankson (HOD of Nurses)
  - Goes to: http://127.0.0.1:8000/hms/hod/scheduling/

STEP 2: Reviews Dashboard
  - Sees: 25 nursing staff
  - Sees: Current week shifts
  - Clicks: "Assign Shift"

STEP 3: Assigns Morning Shift
  - Selects: Nurse Jane Doe
  - Date: Tomorrow
  - Shift: Morning (6 AM - 2 PM)
  - Location: Ward A
  - Duties: "Patient care, vital signs monitoring"
  - Clicks: Save

STEP 4: Assigns More Shifts
  - Repeats for other nurses
  - Different shifts (Morning/Afternoon/Night)
  - Different wards

STEP 5: Staff See Schedules
  - Nurse Jane Doe logs in
  - Goes to: http://127.0.0.1:8000/hms/staff/my-schedule/
  - Sees: "Tomorrow - Morning Shift 06:00-14:00 - Ward A"
  - Knows exactly when and where to work!
