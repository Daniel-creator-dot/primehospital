                        🎯 HOW IT WORKS (SIMPLE)
======================================================================

STEP 1: HOD LOGS IN
-------------------
Example: Vida Blankson (HOD of Nurses) logs in


STEP 2: ACCESS HOD DASHBOARD
-----------------------------
URL: http://127.0.0.1:8000/hms/hod/scheduling/

Sees:
  - Department staff count: 25 nurses
  - This week's shifts: 50 shifts
  - Quick action buttons


STEP 3: ASSIGN A SHIFT
----------------------
Clicks: "Assign Shift" button

Form shows:
  - Staff member (dropdown of all nurses)
  - Date (date picker)
  - Shift type:
    • Morning (6 AM - 2 PM)
    • Afternoon (2 PM - 10 PM)
    • Night (10 PM - 6 AM)
    • Day (8 AM - 5 PM)
    • Custom (set own times)
  - Location (e.g., "Ward A")
  - Duties (e.g., "Patient care, medication rounds")

Fills in:
  - Staff: Nurse Jane Doe
  - Date: Tomorrow
  - Shift: Morning
  - Location: Ward A
  - Duties: Patient care

Clicks: "Assign Shift"

Success: "Shift assigned to Nurse Jane Doe for tomorrow"


STEP 4: STAFF SEES THEIR SHIFT
-------------------------------
Nurse Jane Doe logs in

Goes to: http://127.0.0.1:8000/hms/staff/my-schedule/

Sees:
  TODAY'S SCHEDULE:
    [Blue Box]
    Morning Shift - 06:00 - 14:00
    Location: Ward A
    Duties: Patient care
    Status: Not Checked In

  THIS WEEK'S SHIFTS:
    [Table showing all shifts]
    Tomorrow - Morning - 06:00-14:00 - Ward A
    Friday - Afternoon - 14:00-22:00 - Ward B
    etc.


STEP 5: STAFF CHECKS IN
-----------------------
On shift day, nurse checks in
System tracks attendance
HOD can see who's present
