# ✅ **Leave Approval SMS Template Updated** 📱

## 🎯 **What Changed**

Updated the leave approval SMS to be more **professional and thoughtful**!

---

## 📱 **New SMS Template**

### **Before (Old Message):**
```
Dear Enoch,

Your leave request has been APPROVED!

Type: Annual Leave
Dates: 03/11/2025 to 07/11/2025
Days: 5 day(s)

Enjoy your leave!

PrimeCare Hospital
```

### **After (NEW Message):**
```
Hello Enoch,

Your leave request has been approved.

Type: Annual Leave
Dates: 03/11/2025 to 07/11/2025
Days: 5 working day(s)

Kindly ensure all pending duties are properly handed over 
before your departure. Wishing you a restful and refreshing 
break.

— PrimeCare Management
```

---

## ✨ **Key Improvements**

### **1. Professional Greeting**
- ✅ Changed from "Dear" to "Hello" (more modern)
- ✅ More conversational tone

### **2. Refined Approval Message**
- ✅ Changed "APPROVED!" to "approved" (less shouty, more professional)
- ✅ Calmer, more dignified tone

### **3. Working Days Clarification**
- ✅ Changed "day(s)" to "**working day(s)**"
- ✅ Makes it clear weekends are excluded

### **4. Professional Reminder**
- ✅ Added: "**Kindly ensure all pending duties are properly handed over before your departure**"
- ✅ Reminds staff of their responsibilities
- ✅ Ensures smooth transition

### **5. Thoughtful Closing**
- ✅ Added: "**Wishing you a restful and refreshing break**"
- ✅ Shows care for staff wellbeing
- ✅ Warm and professional

### **6. Enhanced Signature**
- ✅ Changed from "PrimeCare Hospital" to "**— PrimeCare Management**"
- ✅ Em dash (—) adds elegance
- ✅ "Management" shows authority and care
