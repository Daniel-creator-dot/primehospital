## 📊 LEAVE COUNTDOWN FEATURES

**Visual Progress Bars:**
- 🟢 **Green** - You have plenty of leave (>50%)
- 🟠 **Orange** - Getting low (20-50%)
- 🔴 **Red** - Very low (<20%)

**Shows:**
- Annual Leave: X days remaining
- Sick Leave: X days remaining
- Casual Leave: X days remaining

**Example:**
```
Annual Leave: 15 of 21 days
████████████████████░░░░░░ 71% remaining
```

---

## 📱 SMS NOTIFICATIONS

**You'll get SMS when:**
- ⚠️ Leave drops below 5 days (LOW)
- 🚨 Leave drops below 2 days (CRITICAL)
- ❌ Leave is exhausted - 0 days (EXHAUSTED)

**SMS Example:**
```
"WARNING: Your Annual Leave balance is CRITICAL 
(1.5 days remaining). Plan accordingly."
```

**Won't spam you:**
- Only sends once per week
- You can dismiss alerts on your dashboard

## 🔧 FOR HR: CREATE ACTIVITIES

**Quick Setup:**

1. Go to Admin
2. Click "Staff Activities"
3. Click "Add Staff Activity"
4. Fill in:
   ```
   Title: "CPR Training"
   Type: Training Session
   Date: Nov 15, 2025, 9:00 AM
   Location: Training Room 2
   All Staff: ✓
   Is Mandatory: ✓
   ```
5. Save

✅ **All staff now see it on their dashboard!**
