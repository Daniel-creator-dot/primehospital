======================================================================
                        🏥 HOW IT WORKS
======================================================================

WORKFLOW:

STEP 1: Lab completes test
  - Lab staff enters results
  - Test shows in "Completed Today" tab

STEP 2: Check payment status
  - If paid: Shows green "Paid" badge
  - If unpaid: Shows "Send to Cashier" button

STEP 3: Send to cashier
  - Click "Send to Cashier" button
  - Opens cashier page in new tab
  - Patient details pre-filled
  - Test price auto-populated

STEP 4: Cashier processes payment
  - Cashier enters payment method
  - Generates receipt with QR code
  - Receipt emailed to patient
  - Payment synced to accounting

STEP 5: Auto-sync
  - Revenue recorded
  - Journal entries created
  - Ledger updated
  - Reports updated
