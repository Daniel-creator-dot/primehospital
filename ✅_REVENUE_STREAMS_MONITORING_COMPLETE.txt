======================================================================
                        🎯 HOW IT WORKS
======================================================================

AUTOMATIC CATEGORIZATION:

When revenue is created, system checks:
  - Which department?
  - What service type?
  - Automatically categorizes!

EXAMPLE:
  Lab test payment:
    - Service type: Laboratory
    - Department: Laboratory
    - Stream: LAB
    - Amount: GHS 50.00
    - Shows in: Lab revenue card

  Pharmacy sale:
    - Service type: Pharmacy
    - Department: Pharmacy  
    - Stream: PHARM
    - Amount: GHS 120.00
    - Shows in: Pharmacy revenue card
