## 🔍 **How to Use**

### **For HR Managers:**

#### **Viewing Staff Profile:**
1. Go to: `http://127.0.0.1:8000/hms/hr/staff/`
2. Click on any staff member
3. You'll see the complete staff profile with all tabs

#### **Navigating the Profile:**
- Click on tabs to switch between sections
- Each tab shows relevant information
- Statistics are shown at the top
- Quick actions available at the bottom

#### **Understanding Leave History:**
- **Overview Tab** shows current leave balance
- **Leave History Tab** shows all leaves taken
- Statistics show total days and breakdown by type
- Each leave shows dates, type, status, and approver

#### **Reviewing Performance:**
- **Performance Tab** shows all reviews
- Latest review is highlighted at the top
- Average score is calculated automatically
- KPI ratings show detailed performance metrics

#### **Tracking Medical History:**
- **Medical History Tab** shows all medical chits
- See which chits were approved and used
- Track visit creation from chits
- View reasons and symptoms
