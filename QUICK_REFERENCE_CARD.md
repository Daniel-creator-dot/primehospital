## ⚡ QUICK ACTIONS

### For Receptionists:
- **New Patient:** Dashboard → "New Patient" button
- **Book Appointment:** Appointments → "New Appointment"
- **Search Patient:** Patients → Search box

### For Nurses:
- **Record Vitals:** Encounter → "Record Vitals" button
- **View Patients:** Patients → Filter by ward
- **Triage:** Triage Dashboard

### For Doctors:
- **Start Consultation:** Encounter → "Start Consultation"
- **Prescribe:** Consultation → Prescribe tab
- **Order Lab:** Consultation → Lab Tests tab

### For Pharmacists:
- **Dispense:** Pharmacy → Pending Prescriptions
- **Check Stock:** Pharmacy → Stock Management
- **Verify Payment:** Click "Check Payment"

### For Lab Technicians:
- **Process Order:** Laboratory → Pending Orders
- **Enter Results:** Click "Enter Results"
- **Print Report:** Results → "Print Report"

### For Cashiers:
- **Process Payment:** Cashier → Patient Bills
- **Generate Receipt:** After payment
- **View Pending:** Cashier → All Pending
