## 🎯 COMPLETE WORKFLOW

### **Step-by-Step Process:**

**1. Doctor Prescribes** (from consultation)
- Doctor creates prescription order
- Order appears in pharmacy pending queue

**2. Pharmacist Views Order** (pharmacy dashboard)
- Click "Dispense Medication" button
- Modal opens showing:
  - Patient details (name, MRN, age, phone)
  - All prescribed medications
  - Dose, frequency, duration
  - Quantity needed
  - Stock availability ✅ In Stock / ⚠️ Low / ❌ Out
  - Individual prices
  - Total amount

**3. Send to Cashier** (payment)
- Click "Send to Cashier" button
- Opens patient bills page in new tab
- Cashier sees all pending services
- Processes combined payment
- Receipt generated

**4. Verify Payment** (back to pharmacy)
- Click "Verify Payment & Dispense"
- System checks if payment received
- If paid: proceeds with dispensing
- If not paid: shows alert

**5. Dispense Medications** (complete)
- Pharmacist confirms dispensing
- System creates dispensing records
- Order marked as completed
- Accounting synced automatically
- Stock updated

**6. Patient Receives** (finish)
- Patient gets medications
- Has receipt as proof of payment
- Complete audit trail maintained

## 🔐 PAYMENT VERIFICATION

### **How It Works:**
1. Patient goes to cashier
2. Cashier processes payment
3. PaymentReceipt created with service_type='pharmacy_prescription'
4. Pharmacist clicks "Verify Payment & Dispense"
5. System queries PaymentReceipt table
6. If receipt found: allows dispensing
7. If not found: shows alert

### **What's Checked:**
- Payment receipt exists
- For this patient
- Service type = 'pharmacy_prescription'
- Receipt date ≥ prescription date

### **Security:**
- Can't dispense without payment (optional enforcement)
- Complete audit trail
- Who dispensed, when, what
- Payment linked to dispensing record
