## 💰 **COMPLETE WORKFLOW**

### **Step-by-Step: Lab Test Payment**

```
STEP 1: DOCTOR ORDERS LAB TEST
  Doctor: Orders "CBC" for patient John Smith
  System: Creates LabResult
  System: Auto-creates bill (Invoice + LabResultRelease)
  
STEP 2: CASHIER SEES BILL IN DASHBOARD
  Cashier: Opens /hms/cashier/central/
  Dashboard shows:
    🧪 Pending Lab Tests (1)
    - CBC for John Smith (PMC001234) - $25.00
    [Process Payment] ← Button visible
  
  OR Cashier clicks "View All Pending Bills"
  Shows in comprehensive table:
    | Lab | John Smith | PMC001234 | CBC | $25.00 | [Process Payment] |
  
STEP 3: CASHIER PROCESSES PAYMENT
  Cashier: Clicks "Process Payment"
  Form shows:
    - Patient: John Smith (PMC001234)
    - Service: Complete Blood Count (CBC)
    - Amount: $25.00
  
  Cashier enters:
    - Amount: $25.00
    - Payment Method: Cash
  
  Cashier: Clicks "Process Payment & Generate Digital Receipt"
  
STEP 4: SYSTEM AUTO-MAGIC! ✨
  System automatically:
    ✅ Creates Transaction (TXN20251106120530)
    ✅ Creates PaymentReceipt (RCP20251106120530) ← AUTO-GENERATED!
    ✅ Creates QR Code with receipt data
    ✅ Generates QR code PNG image
    ✅ Links receipt to LabResultRelease
    ✅ Updates release status: "ready_for_release"
    ✅ Sends Email with receipt + QR
    ✅ Sends SMS with download link
    ✅ Saves to patient portal
    ✅ Syncs to accounting (Debit Cash / Credit Lab Revenue)
  
STEP 5: RECEIPT DISPLAYED
  Shows receipt with:
    - Receipt Number: RCP20251106120530 ✅ (AUTO-GENERATED!)
    - Patient: John Smith
    - Service: CBC
    - Amount: $25.00
    - QR Code (big, scannable)
  
  [Print Receipt] button available
  
STEP 6: PATIENT NOTIFIED
  Patient receives:
    📧 Email: Beautiful HTML receipt with QR code image
    📱 SMS: "Receipt RCP... View: [link]"
    💻 Portal: Receipt viewable online
  
STEP 7: PATIENT TO LAB
  Patient: Goes to lab
  Patient: Shows phone with QR code (from email/SMS)
  
  Lab Tech: Opens /hms/laboratory/pending-release/
  Lab Tech: Sees patient in "Paid - Ready to Release" section
  Lab Tech: Clicks "Release Results"
  
STEP 8: LAB VERIFIES PAYMENT
  Lab Tech: Scans QR code from patient's phone
  System: Reads QR data
  System: Finds receipt RCP20251106120530
  System: ✅ "Payment Verified!"
  
STEP 9: LAB RELEASES RESULTS
  Lab Tech: Enters who collecting results
  Lab Tech: Clicks "Release to Patient"
  Lab Tech: Prints results
  Lab Tech: Gives to patient
  
STEP 10: COMPLETE! ✅
  Patient: Leaves with results
  Complete audit trail maintained!
```
