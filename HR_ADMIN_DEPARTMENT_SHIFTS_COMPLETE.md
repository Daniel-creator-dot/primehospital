# ✅ HR/Admin Department-Wise Shift Management - COMPLETE

## 🎯 System Overview

A comprehensive department-wise shift management system for HR and Admin that organizes shifts by department for better visibility and monitoring.

---

## 🌐 Access URLs

### For HR/Admin:

**Main Department-Wise View:**
```
/hms/hr/shifts/by-department/
```
- Overview of all departments with shift statistics
- Color-coded cards showing compliance rates
- Quick access to each department's detailed view

**Department Detail View:**
```
/hms/hr/shifts/department/<department_id>/
```
- Detailed shift list for a specific department
- Shows attendance vs scheduled shifts
- Compliance metrics and statistics

**Department Calendar View:**
```
/hms/hr/shifts/department/<department_id>/calendar/
```
- Monthly calendar view of shifts for a department
- Easy navigation between months

---

## 🎨 Features

### 1. **Department Overview Dashboard**
- **All Departments Listed**: Every department shown in organized cards
- **Color-Coded Compliance**: 
  - 🟢 Green: 90%+ compliance (high)
  - 🟡 Yellow: 70-89% compliance (medium)
  - 🔴 Red: <70% compliance (low)
  - ⚪ Gray: No shifts scheduled
- **Quick Statistics**: 
  - Total shifts per department
  - Present/Absent/Late counts
  - Compliance percentage
  - Upcoming shifts count
- **HOD Information**: Shows Head of Department for each department
- **Click to View**: Click any department card to see detailed view

### 2. **Department Detail View**
- **Complete Shift List**: All shifts for selected date
- **Attendance Status**: Present/Absent/Late/On Leave indicators
- **Check-In Times**: Shows actual check-in vs scheduled start time
- **Late Tracking**: Minutes late displayed for each staff
- **Statistics Cards**: Total shifts, present, absent, compliance
- **Upcoming Shifts**: Next 7 days preview
- **Date Navigation**: Easy date selection

### 3. **Department Calendar View**
- **Monthly Calendar**: Full month view of shifts
- **Day-by-Day Breakdown**: See shifts for each day
- **Navigation**: Previous/Next month buttons
- **Today Highlighting**: Current date clearly marked

## 🔍 How to Use

### Step 1: Access Department-Wise View
1. **Log in** as HR Manager or Admin
2. **Navigate** to: `/hms/hr/shifts/by-department/`
   - Or click "Shifts by Dept" button on HR Dashboard
3. **Select Date**: Use date picker to view any day

### Step 2: Review Department Cards
- **Green Cards**: Departments with good compliance (90%+)
- **Yellow Cards**: Departments with medium compliance (70-89%)
- **Red Cards**: Departments with low compliance (<70%)
- **Gray Cards**: Departments with no shifts scheduled

### Step 3: View Department Details
1. **Click** any department card
2. **See** complete shift list with attendance status
3. **Review** compliance metrics
4. **Check** upcoming shifts

### Step 4: Navigate Calendar
1. **Click** "Calendar View" button
2. **Browse** monthly calendar
3. **See** all shifts for the month
4. **Navigate** between months
